Hit0 When sent as a move, will give you another card. If the total value of your hand is less than 21, PlayerMove will fire. If you bust or get 21, you will not receive another PlayerMove event.
Stand1 When a player stands, it will be the next players turn.
DoubleDown2 You can only double down if you have not yet received another card. Doubling down means you receive exactly one card, and your bet amount is doubled. You must have a balance large enough to cover the double down. For example, if a player has a balance of $1,000. If you bet $500, you can double down ($500 x 2 less than balance of $1,000) If you bet $750 and your balance is $1,000, you will receive an error saying they do not have a balance, ($1000 is less than $750 x 2 or $1,500)
PayToSeeDealersCard3 By default, you can only see one of the dealer’s card. If you pay for a dealer’s card, this must be the first thing you do on your turn. After placing this move, it will be your turn again. If you pay to see the dealer’s card, you will only receive 75% of your bet. For example, with a bet of $1,000, if you win, you would only keep $750.
